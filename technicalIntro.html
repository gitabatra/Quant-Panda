Technical Indicators

Technical indicator is a mathematical calculation which is based on the historic price, volume, and/or open interest of a stock/security and used by technical analysts/traders for technical analysis to predict probable future price movements. In technical analysis a stock's trading history is recorded by maintaining volumes traded and price changes in a chart and technical indicators are additions to a chart that helps to predict the market trend.

  • Technical indicator is a mathematical calculation that can be applied to stock's past patterns, like price, volume or even to another technical indicator.
  • It helps the trader to identify the direction of future prices and alert the trader about the market trend.
  • Technical indicators are widely used by traders in the market as they are primarily designed for analysing short-term price movements.Thus, they are not useful for fundamental analysis like business earnings, revenue and profit margins.
